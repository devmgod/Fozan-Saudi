Al Fouzan Maintenance Center

ALF Maintenance Center is specialized in General Maintenance Services and repairs for heavy equipment and Industrial engines as well as provide preventive maintenance on a timely basis depending upon the scope and nature of work, based on Customers requirement. The maintenance center is well equipped with qualified and experienced staff and technicians for the maintenance services. Being well known and distinguished in this field, we have the reputation, particularly on heavy equipment inspection as well as repair and maintenance of all types of industrial engines and equipments of SABIC companies and its affiliates. Moreover we offer special services such as: Hydraulic cylinders, control valves, hydraulic pump, hydraulic motor repairs and trouble shooting.
